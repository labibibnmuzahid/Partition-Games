How to Play

On your turn, you can choose one of two moves:

  • Remove a row: Click on any row to remove it entirely from a fragment.
  • Remove a column: Click on any column to remove it entirely from a fragment.

If removing a row or column splits a fragment into multiple pieces, each piece becomes a separate fragment.

Win condition: The last player able to make a move wins.
