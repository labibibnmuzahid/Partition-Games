How to play

Each fragment is an independent rectangular group of squares.

  • Click a row label (grey bar on the left) to remove the entire row of that fragment.
  • Click a column label (grey bar on top) to remove the entire column of that fragment.

If this causes the shape to split, the resulting parts become separate fragments. The last player who can make a legal move wins.
