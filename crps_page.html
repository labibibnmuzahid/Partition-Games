How to Play

CRPS is a partizan version of CRIS with asymmetric player moves:

  • Player A (Rows): Can only remove entire rows from any fragment.
  • Player B (Columns): Can only remove entire columns from any fragment.

Each fragment is an independent rectangular group of squares. If a move causes a fragment to split, the resulting parts become separate fragments.

Only moves available to the current player will be highlighted. The last player who can make a legal move wins.
