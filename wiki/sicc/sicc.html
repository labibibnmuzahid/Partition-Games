Stirct Impartial Continuous Corner (SICC)

Partizan Integer Partition Combinatorial Game

SICC is a variant of Corner game, where the board is a Young diagram, and the players can only remove a nonempty set of consecutive corners that include either the top right corner or the bottom left corner (or both). Like other games,he player who cannot make a legal move loses the game.
