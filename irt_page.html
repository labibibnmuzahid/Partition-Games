Rules (IRT)

• The diagram is a left-justified stack of rows (a Young diagram).
• On your turn pick any row whose length is at least the length of the row directly underneath (if such a row exists).
• Remove one or more blocks from the right end of that row, leaving at least one block behind.
• The player that removes the last remaining block anywhere on the board wins.
