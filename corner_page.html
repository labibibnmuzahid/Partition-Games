how to play

corner is a young-diagram game where players take turns removing cells from a partition.

  • click anywhere on the board to enter selection mode
  • select which "last cells" to remove from consecutive groups
  • for consecutive rows of the same length, only the last row in each group can lose a cell

the player who removes the last cell wins the game.
