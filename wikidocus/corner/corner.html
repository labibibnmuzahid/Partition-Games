Corner

Integer Parition Combinatorial Game

Let $[r_1, r_2, r_3, \ldots, r_i]$ be an integer parition of a positive integer $n$ in descending order, where $\forall x \in \left(1, 2, \ldots, i\right), r_x \geq 1$. Corner is designed to be played between two players, where the game board will be a Young diagram, representing a partition with previously documented properties. When it's their turn, the player will remove last cell from each of the row; however, in case of consecutive rows of same length, the player will remove a cell only from the last row with identical length. The player who makes the last move wins.
