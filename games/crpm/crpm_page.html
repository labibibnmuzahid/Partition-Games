How to Play

CRPM is a partizan version of CRIM with asymmetric player moves:

  • Player A (Rows): Can only remove entire rows of squares.
  • Player B (Columns): Can only remove entire columns of squares.

Click on the number labels (grey bars) to select a row or column. Only moves available to the current player will be highlighted.

Like CRIM, after each move the divided parts are merged together.
