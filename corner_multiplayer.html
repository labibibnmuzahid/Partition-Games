How to Play Corner - Multiplayer

Take turns removing the last cell from each row according to these rules:

  • For rows that are different lengths, remove the last cell from each row.
  • For consecutive rows of the same length, only remove the last cell from the last row in each group.

Example: If you have rows [5, 4, 4, 3], only rows 1, 3, and 4 lose their last cell.

The player who makes the last move (clears the board) wins the game.

Multiplayer: Wait for your turn and click on the highlighted cells to make your move.
